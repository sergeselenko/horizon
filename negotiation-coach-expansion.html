Adding the Expansion Pack to Your Coach
About 30 seconds — one quick step.

The expansion content needs to be added to your Claude Project so the coach can access the additional scenarios and guided tools.

1
Download the Expansion Pack File
Click the button below to download the expansion pack content as a text file.
2
Upload to Your Claude Project
In your Claude Project settings, click the "+" button in the Files area, select the downloaded file, and save. The 15 scenarios and 3 guided tools become available immediately in any new chat.
What Changes After Setup

Once the expansion file is loaded, three new capabilities are available in every chat:

  • Ackerman Guided Walkthrough — Step-by-step price negotiation method using your real numbers
  • Follow-Up After Silence — Timed sequence builder for when someone goes dark
  • Accusation Audit Builder — Walk through building an accusation audit for any difficult conversation

You can also copy any of the 15 scenario prompts from the Scenarios tab on this portal and paste them into your coach.
