What Each Component Does

Core · $67 Project Instructions (File 1)
The system prompt — the "brain" of the coach. Defines 5 session modes (Quick Coach, Deep Strategy, Communication Drafting, Role-Play Practice, Debrief & Learn). Contains Camp/Voss/Fisher negotiation frameworks, anti-patterns to avoid, communication templates, and a pre-communication checklist. This is what makes Claude behave like a negotiation consultant instead of a generic chatbot.
Core · $67 Knowledge Base (File 2)
The reference library the coach draws from. 11 sections: principled negotiation, executive communication, pitch/proposal design, Cialdini's 6 influence principles, stakeholder management, conflict resolution, written communication, the Ackerman bargaining method (full standalone section), master cheat sheet, and a negotiation prep template. 4,912 words of curated negotiation IP.
Core · $67 Scenario Starter Pack (File 3)
5 ready-to-use scenarios with fill-in-the-blank prompts: salary negotiation, vendor/contractor pricing, business partnership terms, real estate transaction, and client/sales deal. Each includes "What the Coach Will Do" expectations and 3 follow-up prompts. The buyer copies a prompt, fills in their details, and gets coaching tailored to their specific situation.
Bump · +$27 Expansion Pack (File 4)
15 additional scenarios in 3 categories: Advanced Negotiations (multi-party, recovery, hostile counterparty, negotiating from weakness, high-stakes renewal), Specific Contexts (freelancer rates, startup equity, medical bills, divorce assets, landlord-tenant), and Role-Play (Claude plays the counterparty and fights back — hardball buyer, emotional manipulator, silent treatment, nibbler, good cop/bad cop). Plus 3 guided tools: Ackerman Walkthrough, Follow-Up After Silence, Accusation Audit Builder.
Free · Included Notion Upgrade Folder
A bonus folder sitting in the zip for when the buyer is ready. Contains: a PDF explaining what connecting Notion unlocks (persistent memory, Key Person Profiles, running intelligence files), extraction prompts for pulling profile data from emails/transcripts/social media, and profile-building prompts for maintaining counterparty intelligence files. No additional purchase required — this is a seed for future engagement, not a product.
Future · Not Being Sold Notion Connected Tier
If the buyer connects Notion via MCP (a free Claude setting), the coach gets persistent memory: it remembers people across sessions, builds Key Person Profiles, processes transcripts and emails, and keeps running intelligence files. Same Claude Project — no new purchase needed. Whether this tier will eventually be a paid upgrade ($47) or remain free is an open question decided after P1 conversion data comes in.
